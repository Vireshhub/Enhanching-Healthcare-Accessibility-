Video Consultation

Welcome to Our Video Consultation Page

Get the care you need from the comfort of your own home.

How It Works

Our video consultation service allows you to connect with a healthcare professional from the comfort of your own home. Here's how it works:

  1. Schedule your appointment online or by phone.
  2. Receive a confirmation email with a link to join the video consultation.
  1. Click the link at the scheduled time to connect with your healthcare professional.
  2. Discuss your symptoms, ask questions, and receive a personalized treatment plan.

Additional Information

Here are some additional things you should know about our video consultation service:

  • We accept most major insurance plans.
  • You will need a device with a camera and microphone to use the video consultation service.
  • Appointments are available Monday through Friday, 9am to 5pm.
  • If you have any questions or concerns, please contact our customer support team.
